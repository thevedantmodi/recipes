Notes:

    REALLY EASY

Ingredients:

    Pan
    
    Sunflower seed oil
    Jeera

    Hing

    Small gobi
    Golden potatoes

Steps:
    1. Peel potatoes
    2. Dice the potatoes    
    3. Medium heat pan
    4. Add enough oil
    5. Sizzle a pinch of jeera
    6. Add a pinch of hing powder
    7. Add gobi, sautee for a bit
    8. Add aloo, sautee for some more
    9. Mix salt in
    10. Cover it and let it cook, stirring from time to time
    11. Serve
