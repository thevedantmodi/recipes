Notes:

    Easy? chicken dish

Ingredients:

    Greek yoghurt (strained)

    Chicken tenders
    
    Salt
    Pepper

    Dhaniya Powder
    Laal mirchi
    Haldi
    Ginger garlic paste

    Sunflower seed oil


Steps:

    1. Cover chicken in greek yoghurt
    2. Add in decreasing intensity: dhaniya powder, haldi, laal mirchi
    3. Medium heat pan, add enough oil
    4. Cook until chicken is not springy (Rubber tongs for this step, if using
    non stick pan)
