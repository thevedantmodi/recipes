Notes:
Can be served via mug, bag, paper cup, etc.

Ingredients:
Pot
Masala (powder prepared OR grounded with mortar pestle)
Tea Leaves
Ginger


Steps:
- Start the stove
- Place the pot
- Add a good bit of water (depending on servings, think about how much liquid
  there will be)
- Add some milk (again, think about the liquid)
- Add the tea leaves (enough for the amount of liquid present)
- Wait for boil, and stir while this is happening
- Add the masala powder, and SOME ginger
- Serve.
