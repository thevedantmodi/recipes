Notes:

    Should be less runny than the other toor dal

Ingredients:

    Toor dal
    Water

    Jeera
    Hing
    Salt
    Haldi
    Laal mirchi
    One chopped tomato

    Pressure cooker

    Ghee

Steps:

    1. Soak for 30 mins (optional)
    2. Rinse 1/2 cup toor dal four times.
    3. Drain water
    4. Add 3/2 cup water
    5. Add jeera
    6. Add hing
    7. Add salt
    8. Add haldi
    9. Add laal mirchi
    10. Chop one tomato and add
    11. Pressure cook for 4 whistles with 3x water (so 3/2 cup water)
    12. Let pressure cooker cool down
    13. Add 1 tbsp of ghee to whole dal
